# News Intelligence (Holdings)

## Purpose
Provide daily situational awareness for holdings by gathering, collating, and processing material news, with an emphasis on disaster avoidance rather than reactive trading.

## Scope
- Coverage: all holdings, with emphasis on individual stocks, ETFs, and bonds.
- Source priority (highest to lowest):
  1) Official filings
  2) Press releases
  3) News

## Outputs
- On-demand status view of holdings with relevant, recent news context.
- Disaster alerts during waking hours (6:30 am to 9:00 pm Central).
- Alerts include: bankruptcy, fraud, delisting, covenant breach, regulatory action.
- Sleeve-specific signals may be added (e.g., VLCC/Shipping/Offshore Drilling).

## Constraints
- Focus on situational awareness and risk avoidance; do not trigger actions automatically.
- All downstream actions require human approval.
- Recommendations (if any) must present a range of choices with reasoning.
